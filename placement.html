Placement Cell

The placement cell is operated by professionals with commendable work experience from industry and academic field. The cell is fully equipped with telephone lines, fax connection and computers with internet facility.

Objective

The objective of the Institute is to groom the students by conducting ongoing sessions on communication skills, personality development, Presentation Skills & Extempore speech in order to train them for the placements & secure right job for right candidate.

Pedagogy:

The classroom sessions will be highly interactive and participative in nature. In the teaching methodology case studies, viva, group discussion, presentations, mind expanding exercises and perspective building exercises are included. The distinguishing feature of this programme is to provide hands on experience in applying analytical models in our state- of - the - art computer lab. There will be continuous evaluation & if any participant is lagging behind then required coaching & counseling will be provided.
