Rules & Discipline

  • The candidate should strictly obey the instructions given by the Management from time to time.

  • The vehicles should be parked on a place notified and the vehicles parked elsewhere i. e. other than parking zone shall be deflated.

  • Candidate should drive safely inside the campus as well as outside the campus.

  • The Candidate should carry identity card with him/ her regularly and the identity card should be produced when demanded by any authority on the Institute.

  • Any candidate found guilty of an act of misconduct either in the Institute or outside shall be subjected to strict disciplinary action and may be expelled from the Institute.

  • No candidate should cause any damage to the Institute’s property. The damage caused shall be recovered from the candidate immediately.

  • Strict silence should be observed in the library. Loitering in corridors is strictly prohibited.

  • All candidates should read the notices displayed on the notice board regularly and the Institute will not be responsible for the loss or any disadvantage due to negligence of reading notices on the part of the student.

  • The candidates are required to switch off the cell phones inside the institute premises during academic session.

  • Chewing pan, pan masala, any tobacco products and smoking is strictly prohibited on the campus.

  • The above rules of discipline are always subject to change, modification, addition. Omission or alteration as and when the Director feels it fit and proper and the decisions of the Director shall be final.
